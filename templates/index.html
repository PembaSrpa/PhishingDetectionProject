This application helps you identify potentially malicious URLs to protect yourself from phishing attacks. Simply enter a URL to check if it's safe and secure.

Phishing is a form of cyberattack where attackers impersonate trusted entities to steal sensitive information. Be cautious of the following signs:

  • Misspelled URLs or domains
  • Unusual requests for sensitive information
  • Websites lacking HTTPS security
  • Urgent or threatening language
